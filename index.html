About Christ Embassy

Christ Embassy, also known as Believers' LoveWorld Inc., was founded in 1987 by Pastor Chris Oyakhilome D.Sc., D.D. What began as a campus fellowship has grown into one of the largest and most influential ministries in the world.

With a presence in over 242 countries and ministry activities in 571+ languages, Christ Embassy continues to fulfill the Great Commission through church planting, television broadcasting, publishing, healing crusades, and digital evangelism.

The ministry's flagship devotional, Rhapsody of Realities, has surpassed 6.5 billion copies distributed worldwide, making it the most distributed daily devotional on earth.

Christ Embassy Beaumont

Christ Embassy Beaumont is a vibrant, Word-based church community in Beaumont, Alberta, Canada. We are dedicated to raising leaders who are equipped with the Word of God to transform their world.

Our services are held every Sunday at 10:00 AM at the Beaumont Recreational Center. We welcome everyone to experience the love of Christ and the power of His Word in an atmosphere of faith and fellowship.

Under the leadership of Pastor Ernest Osaghae, the church continues to grow as a beacon of light in the community, reaching families and individuals with the message of eternal life.

Our growing family at Christ Embassy Beaumont

Pastor Ernest Osaghae

Branch Pastor, Christ Embassy Beaumont

Pastor Ernest Osaghae is a passionate minister of the Gospel and the branch pastor of Christ Embassy Beaumont. With a deep commitment to the Word of God and a heart for God's people, Pastor Ernest leads the congregation in faith, love, and purpose.

His ministry is characterized by the demonstration of the Spirit's power, heartfelt worship, and practical, life-changing teachings from the Scriptures. Under his leadership, Christ Embassy Beaumont continues to impact lives and communities across Alberta.

Pastor Chris Oyakhilome D.Sc., D.D.

President, Believers' LoveWorld Inc. (Christ Embassy)

Pastor Chris Oyakhilome is the founder and president of Believers' LoveWorld Inc., a dynamic, multifaceted ministry that has transformed millions of lives globally since 1987. He is the author of Rhapsody of Realities, the world's most distributed daily devotional.

Through initiatives like Healing Streams Live Healing Services, LoveWorld TV networks, InnerCity Missions, and the International School of Ministry, Pastor Chris continues to take the message of salvation and divine healing to every nation on earth.

Statement of Faith

1

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — the final authority for faith and conduct in all matters of life.

2

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal and co-eternal.

3

Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, who died for our sins and rose again for our justification.

4

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues and the operation of spiritual gifts today.

5

Divine Healing

We believe divine healing is the privilege of every believer, provided through Christ's atoning sacrifice and available through faith.

6

The Great Commission

We believe every Christian is called to spread the gospel to all nations, making disciples and demonstrating God's power to the world.
